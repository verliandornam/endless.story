Choose Your Adventure

With Endless Story, you can dive into an exciting adventure across various settings. Become a powerful mage in a Fantasy world or the last survivor in a Post-Apocalyptic wasteland — the choice is yours!

Fantasy

Become a member of a Fantasy world inhabited by mythical beings. Immerse yourself in the atmosphere of a magical medieval era and become part of this enchanting world.

Sci-Fi

In the world of science fiction, anything is possible! It’s like magic, but powered by science! Use cutting-edge technology to achieve your goals. Explore and shape the world of a distant future.

Post-Apocalyptic

The world has ended... Or has it? You are one of the last survivors in a post-apocalyptic world. Maybe even the only one. Explore this captivating world and learn how to survive against all odds!

Pirates

Ahoy, landlubber! Ever dreamed of becoming a pirate? Now’s your chance! Climb aboard a pirate ship and set sail in search of hidden treasures. But beware — you’re an outlaw now!

Space Civilization

In the distant future, planet Earth has been destroyed. Now, civilization exists in space. Feel what it’s like aboard a spaceship, or perhaps even discover a new planet for humanity to call home!
